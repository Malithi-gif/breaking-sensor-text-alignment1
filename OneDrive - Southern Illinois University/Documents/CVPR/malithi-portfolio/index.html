About Me

Researcher, educator, and AI security enthusiast.

Profile

I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at Southern Illinois University with a 4.00/4.00 GPA. My work explores how language models can interpret sensor data while remaining secure, private, and reliable in real-world environments such as healthcare, wearable AI, and smart homes.

My current research studies vulnerabilities in LLM/SLM-integrated sensing systems, including data poisoning, adversarial examples, prompt injection, and cross-modal sensor-text misalignment.
